## Original Goal
Build nutrition playbook that fits my labs & routines

## Path to completion
1. Track lab results in a structured spreadsheet (e.g., Google Sheets) with columns for each metric and targets.
2. Consult the American Diabetes Association's "Nutrition Recommendations" and the Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics' "EatRight" guidelines to create a list of specific dietary recommendations tailored to your condition (prediabetes, fatty liver).
3. Categorize foods into protein sources, complex carbs, and healthy fats using the USDA's SuperTracker.
4. Create a meal planning template in Google Sheets that incorporates categorized foods, lab targets, and personal preferences for South Indian vegetarian meals.
5. Calculate daily macronutrient needs based on your lab results and activity level, considering:
6. * Protein: 0.8-1 gram per kg body weight
7. * Complex carbs: 45-55% of total calories
8. * Healthy fats: 20-30% of total calories
9. Set up automated nutrition tracking using a spreadsheet or calculator to monitor progress and identify areas for improvement.
10. Schedule a consultation with a registered dietitian to discuss the initial draft of your nutrition playbook and create a personalized plan.
